Putting an End to Hunger and Food Wastage in Africa

Feed Africa Farms is an Agro-Focused Company solving hunger and food wastage in Africa.

A Brief History

Feed Africa Farms was formed in 2018 when seven young graduates seeking to make their lives and future better came together with one clear goal- provide Food for everyone. It started slowly for them, and the road was not as straight as they hoped, but in all, these men trudged on and learnt from their failures.

FAF has employed over ten persons within the space of one year, and about half a million naira has already been invested for the future.

Hard Work, Teamwork, Sacrifice, Integrity and Accountability- these five cardinal traits have been the bedrock of the agribusiness since its inception. And from all indices, the dreams of those seven men will definitely become a reality for the seven continents of the world.
